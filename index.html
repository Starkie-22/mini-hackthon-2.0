Two Player Trivia Game

Instructions

This is a two-player trivia game where players take turns answering questions from a chosen category.

  • Enter unique names for both players and start the game.
  • Select a category to begin. You can also pick "Random."
  • Players take turns answering 6 questions (2 each of easy, medium, and hard difficulty).
  • Scores are updated based on the difficulty of the questions.
  • The player with the higher score wins at the end.
